# Commercial Licensing

TheMeshProject offers commercial licensing options for organizations that wish to:

- Use documentation or educational content in paid courses
- Integrate TheMeshProject material into commercial products
- Bundle TheMeshProject content with proprietary software
- Use TheMeshProject branding, logos, or visual identity
- Obtain custom licensing terms beyond MIT + CC BY-NC 4.0

To request a commercial license, contact:

contact@themeshproject.com

Please include:
- Your organization name
- Intended use case
- Scope of distribution
- Any required modifications or derivative works

We will respond with available licensing options and terms.
